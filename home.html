The Aurora Healthcare was set up in 2015 and was envisioned to offer competitive medical services in Ngong and its environs. Pathophysiology/Sports clinic

Antenatal services

These services aim to monitor the health and well-being of both the mother
and the developing fetus throughout the duration of the pregnancy.

Post Natal services

These services focus on supporting the physical and emotional well-being of both the mother and the newborn as they transition into the postpartum period
and adjust to the challenges and changes associated with early motherhood and newborn care.

Ultrasound services

ultrasound services involve the use of specialized equipment called an ultrasound machine, which emits high-frequency sound waves and captures the echoes produced as the waves bounce off internal organs, tissues,
and structures. These echoes are then processed by a computer to generate images that can be viewed in real-time on a monitor.

Family planning

refers to the conscious effort by individuals or couples to regulate the number and timing of pregnancies,
as well as the spacing between children, through the use of contraception or other methods.

Weekly Specialists clinics

Refer to medical clinics or outpatient services where specialized healthcare professionals, known as specialists, hold regular appointments or clinics on a weekly basis to provide specialized medical care,
consultations, and treatments to patients with specific health conditions or needs.

Maternity Services

Refer to a range of healthcare services provided to women during pregnancy, childbirth,
and the postpartum period to ensure the health and well-being of both mother and baby.

Vaccines and immunizations(KEPI/Baby friendly)

refers to a specific vaccination schedule or program implemented in Kenya,
often referred to as the KEPI (Kenya Expanded Programme on Immunization)

Minor procedures

refer to medical procedures or interventions that are relatively simple, low-risk, and can be performed in outpatient settings
without the need for general anesthesia or extensive hospitalization
